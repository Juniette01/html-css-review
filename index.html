Filled Cookies

Yield: 250 cookies

Ingredients

Instructions
  • 1 1/4 quart butter (2 1/2 pounds)
  • 1 2/3 tablespoon vanilla
  • 1 1/4 quart sugar (2 1/2 pounds)
  • 5 eggs
  • 3 3/4 quarts flour (3 3/4 pounds)
  • 1/3 cup baking powder (2 ounces)
  • 1 pint evaporated milk
  • 1 cup water, mixed with evaporated milk
  • Jam, fig or other filling
  1. Cream butter with vanilla
  2. Add sugar and continue creaming until light and fluffy
  3. Add eggs
  4. Sift flour, then measure
  5. Resift with baking powder
  6. Add flour alternately with the diluted milk to the creamed butter and sugar mixture, beginning and ending with flour
  7. Chill dough, then roll out and cut
  8. Spread half of each cookie with 1/2 teaspoon jam
  9. Fold over other half and press edges together
  10. Place on greased baking sheets and bake in a moderate oven (350Â° F) 8 to 10 minutes
